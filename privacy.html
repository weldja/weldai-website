Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Weld AI  ·  York, England

Summary: Weld AI installs software on your own server. Your documents never leave your server. We collect only the minimum personal data needed to provide our services.

1. Who we are

Weld AI is a software installation and consulting service based in York, England. We provide private AI document assistant software for small and medium-sized businesses. Contact: hello@weldai.uk

2. What data we collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and company name (when you submit an enquiry)
  • Basic website analytics (page views, general location) via privacy-respecting tools
  • Email correspondence you send to us

We do not collect payment information directly — payments are handled by third-party processors.

3. How the Weld AI software handles your data

The Weld AI software we install operates entirely on your own server. This means:

  • Your documents are stored and processed on your own infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your documents, queries, or conversation history
  • No documents are uploaded to Weld AI servers at any time

When using the Claude API integration, your questions and relevant document excerpts are sent to Anthropic's API to generate answers. Anthropic's Terms of Service explicitly prohibit using API inputs to train their AI models. For full details, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

4. How we use your data

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide our services
  • To send service-related communications (installation updates, support)
  • To improve our website and services

We will never sell your personal data to third parties or use it for unsolicited marketing.

5. Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the services you've requested
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our services, responding to enquiries
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly opted in to communications

6. Data retention

We retain enquiry data for up to 2 years. Email correspondence is retained for up to 6 years for legal and contractual purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, contact: hello@weldai.uk

8. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for basic functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Third-party services

  • Anthropic Claude API — used for AI-powered answers within the installed software. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Email hosting — enquiry emails processed via our email provider

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
